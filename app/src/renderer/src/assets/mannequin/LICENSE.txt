Xiaobaihe removes the original textures and separate eye/eyebrow meshes,
keeps the authored body topology and humanoid skin, and renders the result with
the user's selected whitebox material. The preparation process is reproducible
with scripts/prepare-quaternius-mannequin.mjs.

Xiaobaihe uses the in-place Standard library and Three.js SkeletonUtils
retargeting. The included pose buttons sample Idle_Loop, Sitting_Idle_Loop,
Interact, Walk_Loop and Sprint_Loop while preserving the target mannequin's
bone lengths. Root-motion variants and paid Pro/Source files are not included.
